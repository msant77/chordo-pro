Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

TL;DR — Chordo Pro does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Your chord sheets, settings, and usage stay entirely on your device.

Information We Collect

None. Chordo Pro does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, crash reports, or telemetry of any kind. The app works 100% offline and never contacts any server.

Data Storage

All data — including your song library, settings, tags, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using an on-device SQLite database. This data is never transmitted to any external service.

Third-Party Services

Chordo Pro does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs collecting data in the background.

Network Access

Chordo Pro does not require an internet connection to function. The app may access the network only if you explicitly use features like file import/export via system sharing, which are handled by your operating system — not by Chordo Pro.

Children's Privacy

Since Chordo Pro collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page. Given our commitment to offline-first, privacy-respecting software, we do not anticipate material changes.

Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach us at privacy@chordo.pro or open an issue on GitHub.
